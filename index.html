Biography

Sridipa Dandapat holds a PhD from the Department of Humanities and Social Sciences at IIT Patna. Since September 2024, she has served as Executive Editor of International Research in Children's Literature (Edinburgh University Press). She is currently an Associate with the Quality Reading Material team at Room to Read India, supporting research synthesis, data review, and cross-functional coordination to advance inclusive education across global programs.

She was a Visiting Researcher at TU Darmstadt, consulting on research examining South Asian digital repositories and the role of NGO-led, open-access platforms in enabling readership, representation, and equitable knowledge circulation. She is a recipient of the International Youth Library Fellowship and multiple international research awards.

Her research interests include gender studies and social justice discourse in children's literature, specifically South Asian fiction. Her scholarship appears in leading international journals and edited volumes, and she contributes actively to academic and professional communities through editorial leadership, peer review, and applied research.
