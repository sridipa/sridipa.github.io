About Me

I am a scholar working at the intersection of gender studies, children's literature, and digital humanities, with a focus on South Asian fiction and picturebook archives. I hold a PhD from IIT Patna (Humanities and Social Sciences).

Since September 2024, I serve as Executive Editor of International Research in Children's Literature (Edinburgh University Press). I am currently an Associate with the Quality Reading Material team at Room to Read India, supporting research synthesis and cross-functional coordination to advance inclusive education globally.

I was a Visiting Researcher at TU Darmstadt (Oct 2025), consulting on South Asian digital repositories and NGO-led open-access platforms. I am also a recipient of the International Youth Library Fellowship (Munich) and multiple international research awards.
